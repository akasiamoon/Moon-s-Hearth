A hearty, warming broth perfect for cold evenings. Earthy and grounding.

  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and diced
  • Wild garlic, leeks, and a heavy pour of cream
  • A pinch of salt and cracked black pepper

Simmer over a low hearth fire until the potatoes yield and the broth is thick and fragrant.
